The department divider that structures the front page — a top rule above a serif section title, with an optional right-aligned action.

```jsx
<RuleHeading weight="heavy" action="See all →">Investigations</RuleHeading>
<RuleHeading weight="medium">The Ledger</RuleHeading>
```

`heavy` (vermillion) marks the lead department; `medium` (ink) for the rest; `hairline` for sub-sections.
