We collected every eviction filing in the county since 2016. The paper record tells a story the official numbers left out.

A serif built for screens carries the reading; the measure is held to roughly 65 characters so the eye never loses its place across a line of type. Body copy is set at 17px with generous 1.62 leading.

Sans is reserved for the machinery of the page — labels, navigation, captions and metadata — never for long-form reading.
