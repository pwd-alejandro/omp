Wordmark & identity

A mosaic of pieces that resolves into a whole — set beside Newsreader, the wordmark reads as considered, literary and exact. Two tiles carry vermillion; the rest is ink.

The mark
A 4×4 grid. Twelve tiles are set — nine ink, three vermillion — leaving four gaps. The gaps are the point: the picture is never quite complete until you collect the last pieces. Build on a 9px tile with a 2px channel; never redraw freehand.
